Our Story

The JIET Group of Institutions is a well-established educational group that focuses on providing quality education in the fields of engineering, management, and applied sciences. The group comprises five constituent colleges, all of which are located in the city of Jodhpur. These colleges offer courses under the Bikaner Technical University, Bikaner and are approved by the All-India Council of Technical Education (AICTE). The group was established in 2003 and currently has a student strength of 2500 plus with 13000 plus alumni.

  • Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE)
  • Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology is affiliated with Bikaner Technical University(BTU)
  • Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology are accredited by the NBA.
  • Jodhpur Institute of Engineering and Technology is accredited by NAAC “A” Grade.

About Resonance

Resonance is a techno-cultural festival organized by JIET Group of Institutions that provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and creativity in various cultural, technical and sports events.

Aim

It aims to be an escape from the monotony of everyday life, allowing students to explore new horizons and express themselves in a fun and engaging way.

What Resonance Provides

Resonance provides a platform for students to bring out their inner Shakespeare, Picasso, or Jackson, and showcase their versatility through a gamut of cultural events and activities.
